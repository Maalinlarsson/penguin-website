Penguin Island and retreat

Visit our penguins

When you book your stay at our retreat, you can also visit our penguins. Take a day or two and be impressed how beautiful and magnificent penguins actually are.

You'll se how they live in the wild and also how we help them living a better life.

Meet the newborn penguins

If you are lucky, you get to experience when this years new penguins are born. This is an experience you will never forget. See how the little penguins step into a new world and melt everyone's hearts.
